Printed Cap Seals

Printed cap seals are an excellent choice for businesses aiming to combine secure packaging with effective branding. Available in 2-inch and 3/4-inch sizes and crafted from TFS, Tin Sheet, or Aluminum Sheet, these seals enhance product visibility and customer trust. Whether showcasing a logo, providing safety information, or offering tamper evidence, printed cap seals elevate packaging to a professional and appealing standard.
