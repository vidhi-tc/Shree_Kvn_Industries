Who We Are

Shri KVN Industries is a leader in the manufacturing and export of high-quality drum cap seals. Based in the industrial hub of Ankleshwar, Gujarat, we are proud to serve clients across diverse industries such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food & beverages, and more.

Our mission is to provide world-class sealing solutions that ensure the safety, integrity, and security of your products during storage and transportation. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we strive to build lasting relationships with our clients globally.
